Description of variables in the dataset. All variables except subject and activityFactor
are the mean of several experiments.

subject
	Subject number, 1-30
	
activityFactor
	Name of the activity subject engaged in.

tBodyAcc-mean()-X
	Mean body acceleration mean in X direction in time domain.

tBodyAcc-mean()-Y
	Mean body acceleration mean in Y direction in time domain.

tBodyAcc-mean()-Z
	Mean body acceleration mean in Y direction in time domain.

tBodyAcc-std()-X
	Std. deviation of body acceleration in X direction in time domain.

tBodyAcc-std()-Y
	Std. deviation of body acceleration in Y direction in time domain.

tBodyAcc-std()-Z
	Std. deviation of body acceleration in Z direction in time domain.

tGravityAcc-mean()-X
	Mean gravity acceleration mean in X direction in time domain.

tGravityAcc-mean()-Y
	Mean gravity acceleration mean in Y direction in time domain.

tGravityAcc-mean()-Z
	Mean gravity acceleration mean in Z direction in time domain.

tGravityAcc-std()-X
	Std. deviation of gravity acceleration in X direction in time domain.

tGravityAcc-std()-Y
	Std. deviation of gravity acceleration in Y direction in time domain.

tGravityAcc-std()-Z
	Std. deviation of gravity acceleration in Z direction in time domain.
